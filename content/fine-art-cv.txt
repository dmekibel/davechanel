------------------------------------------------------------
STATEMENT
------------------------------------------------------------
Russian-Israeli artist working in the space between digital
nostalgia, mythology, religion, and art history. The visual
world of Windows XP, 90s television, and the early 2000s
internet is the foundation of how I see and how I make.
Nostalgia isn't a theme in the work, it's the whole frame.

The practice blends pop culture, mythological references,
and personal memory into a style I call Pop Renaissance:
high and low, old and new, sacred and secular collapsed
into one image.
