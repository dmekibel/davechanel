Hi. I'm David Mekibel.

A Russian-Israeli artist working in the space between
digital nostalgia, mythology, religion, and art history.

I've been making computer-based art since I was a kid,
shaped by the visual world of Windows XP, 90s television,
and the early 2000s internet. Those colors, textures, and
moods became the foundation of how I see and how I make.
Nostalgia isn't a theme in the work, it's the whole frame.

I trained as a 3D artist (BVA, Emerson College, 2018) and
worked professionally in 3D and motion. The practice has
grown more painterly and symbolic over the years, blending
pop culture, mythological references, and personal memory
into a style I call Pop Renaissance.
